## 🚀 My Flagship Project — Future With Farhan (FWF)

<div align="center">

[![FWF](https://img.shields.io/badge/🎓%20Future%20With%20Farhan-Pakistan's%20%231%20Student%20Platform-1A3C6E?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/farhanalikalyani/FutureWithFarhan-Web)

</div>

> **Pakistan's most comprehensive student success platform** helping students prepare for MDCAT, ECAT, NTS, CSS, find scholarships, explore universities, and build their dream careers.

### ✨ Features:
- 📚 Notes, MCQs, Past Papers & Video Lectures
- 🏛️ University Hub with Merit Calculator
- 🤖 AI Mentor powered by Claude AI
- 🎯 Career Roadmaps & Internship Alerts
- 🎓 Scholarship Finder
- 📅 Student Planner with 6 tools
- 🔐 Digital Student Locker
- 👥 Student Community
- 🌟 Opportunity Hub
